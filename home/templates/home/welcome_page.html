Product Ideas -> Built Fast




Many product and technical teams struggle at the start of a new project to establish clear requirements and even clearer lines of communication. Initially they need help deciding on many aspects or the project beyond just documenting a solution and agreeing on a timeline, they also need to choose and manage remote and local teams, plan functionality and agree on release dates.

Buildly is a combined product and technical team platform focused on how to make some of the most important parts of building a product easier. The time consuming and frustrating discovery phase and budgeting, as well as establishing solid and consistent communication protocols and release planning regardless of the tools you use. We use a combination of wizard like data collection algorithms for product description and categorization, combined with a set of AI and ML data models and example data sets to translate non-technical requirements into technical issues or tickets gathered into planned releases to provide solid estimates that learn and adapt from feedback to speed up processes and improve overall team performance in any workplace, but especially for software and cloud based products.
