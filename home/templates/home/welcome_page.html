Open Build helps small business and non-profits benefit from open source software, and connect with teams of developers.

We recruit, hire and educate software developers from all over the world to use open source software, learn best practices and processes to build better software and build there own agencies and companies to help benefit there local communities.

If your interested in learning more about how open source and open build can help your company or organization. Get in touch with us.

  • Open Build can help you get your business up and running with cloud native open source software.

  • Start your own company, using free and open source tools and with affordable open source developers.

  • Our partner matching service, we will help you find a developer or development team to build your application.

{% trans "STEM Teaching and Training" %}

With Open Build being an active member in the Open Source community, we are deeply passionate about contributing to the spread of learning and knowledge of Open Source technologies, both software and hardware in our local communities.

Our developers, technical specialists, and analysts volunteer their time to work with local organizations and schools to teach STEM based courses in their local communities.
